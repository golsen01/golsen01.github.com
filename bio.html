Biography

Miss Grace Olsen is the mortal daughter of the fallen god Thor and Xena the warrior princess. She was raised in a small village in the Himalayas of Tibet by a sacred order of Shaolin monks and trained in the martial arts in order to fulfill the prophecy of The Chosen One! Journeying across the many kingdoms of the Earth she sought out the entrance to Ragnarok where her father's killer and malevolent ruler of the Outerworlds lay. She braved perils that were so perilous that they must remain untold for to describe them would be a peril for the sanity of anyone told of them. Having ascended Mount Peril, and reaping her vengeance from the King of the Outerworlds, she returned to the now peaceful mortal realm. She currently attends Tufts University School of Engineering seeking a degree so she can "take it easy doing normal people things for a while."
